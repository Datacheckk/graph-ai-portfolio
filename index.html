About

Why DataCheck exists

DataCheck was developed during the Graph Network GenAI training to make public health data more understandable, accessible, and actionable for everyone ‚Äì regardless of technical background.

The DataCheck platform

DataCheck provides an easy-to-use online platform that centralises public health data and turns it into intuitive visual dashboards for quick, evidence-based decisions.

  • Combine routine data from multiple sources into one clean view.
  • Surface trends and outliers that matter for programme performance.
  • Support quick briefs for review meetings, donor reports, and supervision visits.

The DataCheck Bot

The DataCheck Bot is a conversational assistant that makes Monitoring & Evaluation concepts less intimidating for beginners and busy teams.

  • Explains indicators, definitions, and calculations in simple language.
  • Supports interpretation of charts and tables so teams can act with confidence.
  • Helps navigate reporting frameworks and routine data questions on demand.
